Explanation 1:
This paper outlines how important and influencial exercise can be for relieving symptoms of depression
Evidence 2:
" In some studies moderate exercise can be as effective in reducing anxiety as meditation"

Meditation is extremely effective in reducing anxiety and exercise has been shown to be as effective.

Root Causes #2

In this current internet age many people have a poor body image and dont feel stressed about it.
Evidence 1:
"Exercise has a moderate reducing effect on state and trait anxiety and can improve physical self-perceptions and in some cases global self-esteem. Also there is now good evidence that aerobic and resistance exercise enhances mood states."
Explanation 1:
There has been sufficient evidence that suggests a correlation between exercise and imporvement in body image and reduced stress levels.
Evidence 2:
"Real and Colleagues found that exercise has a positive effect on body image and depression for both men and women"
Explanation 2:
Through their study they found that men and women became more productive in and felt more confident in themselves after participating in moderate amounts of excersie for prolonged amounts of time.

Implications for both Root Causes

There have been significant credible sources providing
in depth research that provides clear evidence about the extent of exercise impacting people depression, anxiety and body image. Increasing the amount of exercise you do will both increase your health and mental health.
